Testimonials

Viraj Dhamdhere

IT Engineering, 2011 Batch

I joined PCCOE in 2008, like most of the people at that time it was not my first choice also it was not known "Brand", I still remeber first day where infrastructure, Asthetics and everything was to the minimal. But progress is synonym for PCCOE. I have seen its growth and it's at the cost of lot of dedication, Hard work and some very right desicions. Discipline was always an important part of PCCOE and at some time it felt like a School of Engineering. But this accounted as a big reason for its success.
All required facilities like Computer Labs, Libraries, Study Rooms, Mess, Hostels are well equipped and sufficient but distinguished factor is efforts provided by college to get their students employed. I remember doing preplacement classes and realized its importance when I saw stundents from other colleges. There are many extracurricular activities like NSS, Sports, Cultural events, Elections in right proportion to keep you entertained still having great focus on exams. If you wish to pursue cultural career while doing your graduation then its not the right place for you. Finance is a huge factor to get graduated and PCCOE excceeds expectation there as it was one of the most reasonable college at that time. As i said at the start , PCCOE was not the first choice for me, but at the end of 4 years i realised, it was the best choice i made.
Having said that, I truly believe that PCCOE deserves a great credit of what I am today Professionally.
